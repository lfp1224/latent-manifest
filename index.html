THE PHILOSOPHY

Shoot Blind

LATENT simulates a real film camera. Every frame you shoot is saved as a latent image — sealed, invisible, just like real film. You can't preview what you've captured.

Develop in the Dark

MANIFEST is your digital darkroom. Load your exposed rolls, choose a film stock simulation, and develop. Only then do your photos emerge — with grain, tone, and character.

The Anticipation is the Point

The separation between shooting and seeing is intentional. It slows you down, makes each frame deliberate, and turns the reveal into a moment worth waiting for.

PRIVACY

100% On-Device

All photos, film rolls, and developed images are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

No Account Required

Neither LATENT nor MANIFEST requires sign-in, registration, or any form of account. Your data belongs to you.

No Tracking

We don't collect analytics, usage data, or any personal information. What you shoot stays between you and your camera.
